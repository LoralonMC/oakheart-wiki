# Discord

Join the Oakheart community on Discord! Chat syncs between the game and Discord so you can stay connected even when not playing.

---

## Join Link

**discord.gg/Oakheart**

---

## What You Can Do on Discord

- **Chat** — Messages sync between Discord and in-game chat
- **Get Support** — Open tickets for staff help with issues
- **Report Problems** — Report griefing, bugs, or rule violations
- **Appeal Bans** — Submit ban appeals for review
- **Server News** — Get announcements about updates and events
- **Community** — Connect with other players

---

## Support Tickets

Need help? Open a support ticket on Discord for:

- Reporting griefing or theft
- Reporting cheaters
- Claiming abandoned builds
- Bug reports
- General questions staff can help with

---

## Chat Sync

In-game chat and Discord chat are connected:
- Messages sent in-game appear on Discord
- Messages sent on Discord appear in-game
- Use this to chat with players even when offline from the server

# Discord

Join the Oakheart community on Discord for chat, support, and news.

**{{discord}}**

---

## Why Join

- **In-game chat bridge** — Talk to players in-game from Discord (and vice versa) in `#ingame-chat`
- **Support tickets** — Get staff help for griefing, bugs, appeals, or general questions
- **Vote reminders** — Subscribe to a daily ping so you don't forget to vote
- **Player community** — Builds showcase, screenshots, player shops, land recruiting, suggestions, off-topic chat
- **Server news** — Announcements, events, changelogs, and sneak peeks

---

## Linking Your Minecraft Account

To send messages in `#ingame-chat` (read-only without linking), connect your Discord and Minecraft accounts:

1. In-game, run `/discord link`
2. You'll receive a code
3. DM that code to the Oakheart bot on Discord
4. Done — you're linked, and the **Linked Account** role appears in Discord

Linking is also required to submit a staff application.

## Key Channels

**Information**
- `#announcements` — Big news, launches, events
- `#vote-links` — Direct links to all 5 vote sites with per-vote reward info
- `#changelogs` — Patch notes and system tweaks
- `#sneak-peeks` — Staff teasers for upcoming features

**Community**
- `#general` — Main chat
- `#off-topic` — Non-Oakheart conversation
- `#media` — Memes, screenshots, fun stuff
- `#suggestions` — Submit ideas as a chat message; the bot reposts them as voteable embeds

**In-Game**
- `#ingame-chat` — Bridged to in-game chat (requires Linked Account to send)
- `#trading` — Player shop announcements and trade requests
- `#player-warps` — Showcase your `/warp`
- `#land-recruiting` — Find co-op partners for your Lands claim
- `#hall-of-fame` — Auto-posted celebrations of rare drops and milestones
- `#questions` — Per-question forum threads with topic tags

**Creative**
- `#build-showcase` — Big builds, multi-image showcase posts
- `#art` — Player art, any creative output welcome

**Voice channels** — Cottagecore-themed rooms: Blossom, Oakgrove, Wheatfield, Hearth

## Self-Assign Notification Roles

Pick up notification roles via Discord's Channels & Roles onboarding to get pinged about things you care about:

- **`ping-announcements`** — Server announcements
- **`ping-events`** — Lotteries, contests, build competitions
- **`ping-voting`** — Daily vote reminder
- **`ping-changelog`** — Patch notes and updates

---

## Reporting Issues

Open a support ticket via the `#tickets` channel and pick the category that fits:

- **In-game Support** — gameplay questions, bug reports, stuck-player issues
- **Player Report** — griefing, harassment, rule-breaking
- **Punishment Appeal** — appeal a ban or mute
- **Billing** — purchase or refund issues
- **Bug Report** — technical issues with the server or plugins

Staff will respond as soon as we can.

# Community Guidelines

Oakheart SMP is a community-focused survival server emphasizing player interaction, physical shops, and collaborative gameplay. We aim to create a welcoming medieval cottagecore village atmosphere.

---

## Core Values

- **Community First** — Support fellow players and build connections
- **Fair Play** — No cheating, exploiting, or unfair advantages
- **Respect** — Treat everyone with kindness and consideration
- **Creativity** — Build, trade, and create unique experiences
- **Physical Presence** — Visit shops, explore bases, interact in-world

---

## Quick Rules

1. **Be respectful** — No harassment, hate speech, slurs, or bullying
2. **No cheating** — No X-ray, hacking, duping, or exploiting (= permanent ban)
3. **No griefing** — Don't break/steal from others' builds (claimed or unclaimed)
4. **Claim your builds** — Use `/lands create <name>` then `/lands claim`
5. **Trade safely** — Use `/trade <player>` for secure trades
6. **Respect the economy** — Don't exploit shop limits or raid cooldowns
7. **PvP is opt-in only** — Use `/pvp` to enable consensual combat
8. **Report issues** — Open a ticket on Discord

---

## Respect & Behavior

**DO:**
- Be kind and respectful to all players
- Use appropriate language in public chat
- Help new players learn the server
- Report rule violations to staff

**DON'T:**
- Use slurs, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Harass, bully, or target other players
- Spam chat or commands
- Advertise other servers

**Consequences:** Warning → Mute (1hr-permanent) → Ban

---

## Griefing & Theft

All claimed land is protected. **But griefing unclaimed builds is still against the rules.**

**DO:**
- Respect all territory (claimed or not)
- Ask permission before building near others
- Report griefing immediately via Discord

**DON'T:**
- Grief, destroy, or modify others' builds
- Steal from chests or containers
- Kill other players' pets or animals
- Build offensive structures

**Consequences:** Rollback + Ban (7 days to permanent)

---

## Cheating & Exploits

**Consequences:** Permanent ban (no appeals for hacking/duping)

**DO:**
- Play fairly using vanilla Minecraft mechanics
- Report bugs and exploits to staff immediately
- Use approved client-side mods

**DON'T:**
- Use hacked clients, X-ray, or cheat mods
- Exploit bugs or glitches
- Dupe items or currency
- Use alt accounts to bypass limits
- Use macros/autoclickers for AFK farming

### Approved Mods

- Optifine/Sodium (performance)
- Litematica (schematic viewer only, no printer)
- MiniMap mods (no entity radar, no cave mapping)
- Shaders
- Inventory tweaks/sorting
- Simple Voice Chat

{% hint style="info" %}
When in doubt about a mod, ask staff before using it.
{% endhint %}

---

## Economy & Trading

**DO:**
- Trade fairly and honor agreements
- Use `/trade` for safe player trades
- Respect shop prices and stock limits

**DON'T:**
- Scam or mislead other players
- Exploit shop stock limits or cooldowns
- Create "emerald generators" via villager exploits
- Trade real money for in-game items (except official store)

**Consequences:** Warning → Trade restrictions → Temp ban → Economy reset + ban

---

## Claims & Land Use

**DO:**
- Claim all builds you want protected
- Leave 64+ blocks between your claims and others

**DON'T:**
- Build offensive structures (hate symbols, NSFW builds)
- Claim around others to box them in
- Bypass claim protections

**Inactive Claims:** Claims inactive for 90+ days may be removed after staff review.

---

## Chat Rules

**DO:**
- Keep chat friendly and welcoming
- Use English in public chat
- Use `/msg <player>` for private conversations

**DON'T:**
- Spam chat or commands
- CAPS LOCK SPAM
- Discuss controversial topics
- Bypass chat filters

---

## PvP

**Default:** PvP is disabled

Use `/pvp` to enable consensual combat. Only PvP with consenting players.

**DON'T:**
- Kill players without consent
- Trap players in PvP zones
- Abuse PvP mechanics to grief

---

## Redstone & Lag

**DO:**
- Build efficient redstone contraptions
- Turn off farms when not in use
- Report lag sources to staff

**DON'T:**
- Build lag machines
- Create massive entity farms
- Use excessive hoppers or redstone clocks

**Consequences:** Warning → Farm removal → Temp ban

---

## Punishment Guidelines

### Mute Durations
1. 1 hour
2. 6 hours
3. 24 hours
4. 7 days
5. Permanent

### Ban Durations
- **Minor** (chat spam, accidental grief): 1-3 days
- **Moderate** (theft, intentional grief, scamming): 7-30 days
- **Major** (hacking, duping, severe harassment): Permanent

### Appeals
Bans 7+ days can be appealed on Discord. Permanent bans for hacking/duping are typically not appealable.

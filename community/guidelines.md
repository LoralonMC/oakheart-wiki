## Discord

Join the Oakheart community on Discord: **discord.gg/Oakheart**

### What You Can Do on Discord

- **Chat** — Messages sync between Discord and in-game chat
- **Get Support** — Open tickets for staff help with issues
- **Report Problems** — Report griefing, bugs, or rule violations
- **Appeal Bans** — Submit ban appeals for review
- **Server News** — Get announcements about updates and events

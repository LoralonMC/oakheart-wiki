# Server Rules

Oakheart SMP is a community-focused survival server. These rules exist to ensure everyone has a positive experience. Please read them carefully.

---

## Quick Rules

**New to Oakheart? Here's the essential version:**

1. **Be respectful** — No harassment, hate speech, slurs, or bullying
2. **No cheating** — X-ray, hacking, duping = permanent ban
3. **No griefing** — Don't steal or destroy others' builds
4. **Claim your builds** — Use `/lands create` and `/lands claim`
5. **Trade safely** — Use `/trade <player>` for secure exchanges
6. **PvP is opt-in** — Use `/pvp` to enable consensual combat
7. **Report issues** — Open a ticket on Discord

---

## 1. Respect & Behavior

### Do
- Be kind and respectful to all players
- Use appropriate language in public chat
- Help new players learn the server
- Report rule violations to staff
- Keep disputes civil and private

### Don't
- Use slurs, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Harass, bully, or target other players
- Spam chat or commands
- Advertise other servers
- Share personal information (yours or others')
- Bypass chat filters or evade mutes

### Consequences
Warning → Mute (1hr → 6hr → 24hr → 7 days → Permanent) → Ban

---

## 2. Griefing & Theft

All claimed land is protected. Griefing **unclaimed** builds is still against the rules.

### Do
- Respect all claimed territory
- Ask permission before building near others (64+ blocks)
- Report griefing immediately via Discord ticket
- Claim your builds to protect them

### Don't
- Grief, destroy, or modify others' builds
- Steal from chests, containers, or item frames
- Kill or harm other players' pets/animals
- Loot abandoned builds without staff permission
- Build offensive or inappropriate structures
- Use lava/water to grief around claims

### Consequences
Rollback + Ban (7 days → Permanent, depending on severity)

---

## 3. Cheating & Exploits

### Do
- Play fairly using vanilla mechanics
- Report bugs and exploits to staff immediately
- Use approved client-side mods (see list below)

### Don't
- Use hacked clients, X-ray, or cheat mods
- Exploit bugs or glitches for advantage
- Dupe items or currency
- Use alternate accounts to bypass limits
- Abuse mechanics for unintended advantages
- Use macros/autoclickers for AFK farming

### Approved Client Mods
- **Performance:** Optifine, Sodium, Lithium
- **Visual:** Shaders, custom textures
- **Building:** Litematica (schematic viewer only, NO printer)
- **Maps:** Minimap mods (NO entity radar, NO cave mapping)
- **Utility:** Inventory sorting, Simple Voice Chat
- **Other QoL mods** — When in doubt, ask staff first

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Hacking = Permanent Ban**
No appeals for X-ray, hacking, or duping. This is non-negotiable.
{% endhint %}

---

## 4. Economy & Trading

### Do
- Trade fairly and honor agreements
- Use `/trade <player>` for safe trades
- Respect shop prices and stock limits
- Report scams or unfair trades

### Don't
- Scam or mislead other players
- Exploit shop stock limits or cooldowns
- Attempt to manipulate the economy
- Create "emerald generators" or exploit villagers
- Trade real money for in-game items (except official store)

### Spawn Shop Limits
Each shop has per-player daily limits. These reset automatically. Don't attempt to bypass them with alt accounts.

### Consequences
Warning → Trade restrictions → Temp ban → Economy reset + Permanent ban

---

## 5. Claims & Land Use

### Do
- Claim all builds you want protected
- Leave 64+ blocks distance from other players' claims
- Contact staff for land disputes

### Don't
- Build offensive structures (hate symbols, NSFW, etc.)
- Build structures to harass or target players
- Claim around others to box them in
- Bypass claim protections with exploits

### Inactive Claims
Claims inactive for 90+ days may be removed by staff. We'll attempt to contact you first.

---

## 6. Player Shops & Warps

### Do
- Create legitimate shops with fair prices
- Use clear warp names and categories
- Keep shop areas accessible
- Update stock regularly

### Don't
- Create fake/scam shops
- Use warps to trap or kill players
- Set warps to inappropriate locations
- Create misleading warp names

### Shop Limits
Based on vote/store rank. Higher ranks = more shops allowed.

---

## 7. Chat & Communication

### Do
- Keep chat friendly and welcoming
- Use English in public chat (other languages in /msg)
- Use `/msg <player>` for private conversations

### Don't
- Spam chat or commands
- USE EXCESSIVE CAPS OR FORMATTING
- Discuss controversial topics (politics, religion)
- Share links without permission
- Bypass chat filters

### Chat Features
- Link items in chat with `[item]`
- Discord chat syncs with in-game chat

### Consequences
Warning → Mute (increasing duration) → Temp ban

---

## 8. Redstone & Server Performance

### Do
- Build efficient redstone contraptions
- Turn off farms when not in use
- Report lag sources to staff

### Don't
- Build lag machines intentionally
- Create massive entity farms
- Use excessive hoppers or redstone clocks
- Build auto-clickers or macro farms

### Consequences
Warning → Farm removal → Temp ban (if intentional)

---

## 9. PvP & Combat

**PvP is disabled by default.** Both players must opt-in.

### Do
- Only PvP with consenting players (both use `/pvp`)
- Return items after friendly fights (if agreed)
- Keep PvP in appropriate areas

### Don't
- Kill players without consent
- Trap players in PvP zones
- Use PvP to harass or grief
- Abuse PvP toggle mechanics

### Consequences
Warning → Temp ban → Permanent ban

---

## Punishment System

### Warning System
Most first offenses result in a logged warning.

### Mute Durations
1. 1 hour
2. 6 hours
3. 24 hours
4. 7 days
5. Permanent

### Ban Durations
- **Minor** (chat spam, accidental grief): 1-3 days
- **Moderate** (theft, intentional grief, scamming): 7-30 days
- **Major** (hacking, duping, severe harassment): Permanent

### Appeals
- Bans 7+ days can be appealed on Discord
- Permanent bans for hacking/duping are typically not appealable
- Staff will review evidence before decisions

## Staff & Moderation

### How to Contact Staff
- **Discord:** Open a support ticket
- **In-game:** Report in chat if urgent

### What Staff CAN Do
- View chat logs
- Inspect block changes
- Restore inventories
- Issue bans/mutes
- Teleport to investigate

### What Staff CANNOT Do
- Give free items or currency
- Bypass economy systems for players
- Share confidential player information
- Abuse permissions for personal gain

Staff are held to higher standards and can be demoted for rule violations.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use client mods?**
A: Yes, see the approved list above. When in doubt, ask staff.

**Q: What if someone griefs my unclaimed build?**
A: It's still against rules. Report via Discord. Always claim to be safe!

**Q: Is PvP allowed?**
A: Only between players who both have `/pvp` enabled.

**Q: How do I report a rule breaker?**
A: Open a Discord ticket with coordinates and details.

**Q: Can I appeal my ban?**
A: Bans 7+ days can be appealed. Hacking bans are not appealable.

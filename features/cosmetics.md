The [Crystal Trader](../traders/crystal-trader.md) offers exclusive cosmetic items including:

- **Cottage Armor Set** — Custom-textured leather armor
- **Cottagecore Tools** — Themed tool skins
- **Decorative Items** — Plushies, lanterns, and more

These items are only available through Crate Crystals.

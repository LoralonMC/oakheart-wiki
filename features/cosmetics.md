# Cosmetics

Oakheart offers a variety of cosmetic items and features to personalize your experience. All cosmetics are purely visual and provide no gameplay advantages.

---

## Cosmetic Types

### Chat Tags

Unlock special tags that appear before your name in chat. Tags are equipped through a menu and can be swapped anytime.

**How to get them:**
- Vote Crate (Exclusive Vote Chat Tag — Legendary rarity)
- Crystal Trader exclusives
- Store rank perks (Phoenix rank)
- Special events

### Login/Logout Messages

Custom join and leave messages based on your rank or unlocked cosmetics. Higher ranks and special unlocks display unique messages when you connect or disconnect.

### Plushies

Decorative custom items for your base. Plushies are 3D-modeled collectibles you can place and display.

**Available plushies:**
- Steve Plushie
- Alex Plushie
- Zombie Horse Plushie
- Ocelot Plushie
- Tropical Fish Plushie
- Creaking Plushie (Rare)

**How to get them:** Vote Crate drops (Uncommon-Rare rarity)

### Figures

Custom 3D mob figures for decorating your builds. Larger and more detailed than plushies.

**Available figures:**
- Zombie Horse Figure
- Endermite Figure
- Snow Golem Figure
- Elder Guardian Figure (Rare)

**How to get them:** Vote Crate drops (Uncommon-Rare rarity)

### Trading Cards

Collectible trading card items. Open booster packs to build your collection and trade with other players.

**How to get them:** Starter Booster Packs from the Vote Crate (Common rarity)

### Pets

Companion pets that follow you around with custom 3D models. See the full [Pets](pets.md) page for details.

### Skins

Cosmetic player skins and overlays to customize your appearance.

## Where to Find Cosmetics

### Silkthread the Tailor

**Location:** Cosmetics Shop at spawn

Visit Silkthread for cosmetic options and to browse available customizations.

### Crystal Trader

The [Crystal Trader](../traders/crystal-trader.md) offers **exclusive cosmetics** only available through Crate Crystals:

- **Cottage Armor Set** — Custom-textured leather armor
- **Cottagecore Tools** — Themed tool skins
- **Decorative Items** — Exclusive plushies, lanterns, and more

These items cannot be obtained any other way.

### Crates

Many cosmetics drop from crates:

| Source | Cosmetics Available |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Vote Crate** | Plushies, Figures, Trading Card Packs, Chat Tag |
| **Paid Crates** | Additional cosmetics per tier |
| **Seasonal Crate** | Limited-time exclusive cosmetics |

### Store Ranks

Each [store rank](../ranks/store-ranks.md) includes an exclusive pet companion:

| Rank | Exclusive Pet |
|------|--------------|
| Hedgehog | Hedgehog Pet |
| Squirrel | Squirrel Pet |
| Otter | Otter Pet |
| Owl | Owl Pet |
| Phoenix | Phoenix Pet |

# Quests

The village folk at spawn have work that needs doing. Every day they pin fresh requests to the **Village Quest Board**: gather this, catch that, delve for the other. Take one on, head out into the world, and bring the results back to whoever asked. It's the cozy daily loop of Oakheart, and it pays.

{% hint style="info" %}
Quests are entirely **optional** and refresh every day, so there's no keeping up or falling behind. Do one when the mood takes you, or clear the whole board for a bonus. Either way, tomorrow brings a fresh set.
{% endhint %}

---

## The Quest Board

Find the **quest board at spawn** and interact with it to see what the village needs today. You can carry:

- **3 daily requests** — smaller tasks, refreshed every day
- **1 weekly village project** — a bigger, multi-part community effort

Accepting a request adds it to your journal. There's no command for it: the board is a real place at spawn, so wander over and give it a click.

---

## How a Quest Works

1. **Accept** a request at the board.
2. **Complete** it out in the world — mine the ore, catch the fish, whatever was asked.
3. **Hand it in** with the villager who posted it. Each one has their own voice, and their own way of thanking you.

Your progress is tracked automatically as you play. When a request is ready, the villager who posted it will have a hand-in waiting.

## Rewards

Every request pays out in:

- **Emeralds** — more for the tougher asks
- **Experience**
- A **Bounty Satchel** — a bundle of surprises (right-click to open)

### Full Clear Bonus

Clear **all three** of your daily requests in a single day and the village rewards the effort with a **Gilded Bounty Satchel**: the richer variant. Once per day.

### Weekly Village Projects

Elder Oakworth's weekly projects are grander things: bigger, multi-part tasks that take the week to finish, and pay the most (including Gilded Bounty Satchels). The weekly project resets every **Monday**.

---

## Bounty Satchels

Every request sends you off with a **Bounty Satchel** — a sealed bundle you right-click to open. What's inside is always a surprise, and never the same twice. Half the fun is finding out.

- **Bounty Satchel** — the standard reward, from any request
- **Gilded Bounty Satchel** — the richer variant, from full clears and weekly projects

---

## Keeping Score

Your questing is tracked on the [website](https://oakheart.net):

- **Leaderboards** — Daily, Weekly, Full Clears, and lifetime Total
- **Profile card** — your quest stats, right on your player profile

## Playing on Bedrock

Everything works on Bedrock. Since Bedrock doesn't handle the villager dialogue menus well, interacting with a villager there **hands in a ready quest, or opens their shop, straight away** — no menus to fuss with. Accept quests at the board just as you would on Java.

# Store Ranks

Support the server and get instant access to perks with Store Ranks! Each rank gives you the same benefits as its equivalent Vote Rank, plus exclusive bonuses and a unique pet companion.

---

## Store Rank Tiers

| Rank | Price | Equivalent Vote Rank | Pet |
|------|-------|---------------------|-----|
| **Hedgehog** | $10 | Rank 5 (Bud) | Hedgehog companion |
| **Squirrel** | $25 | Rank 10 (Root) | Squirrel companion |
| **Otter** | $50 | Rank 15 (Willow) | Otter companion |
| **Owl** | $100 | Rank 20 (Yggdrasil) | Owl companion |
| **Phoenix** | Subscription | Requires Owl (cosmetic only) | Phoenix companion |

{% hint style="info" %}
**Cumulative pricing** ‚Äî If you own Hedgehog ($10) and want Squirrel, you only pay $15 more (not the full $25).
{% endhint %}

## All Commands by Rank

### Hedgehog ($10)

All commands from Vote Ranks 1-5:
- `/cartographytable` ‚Äî Open cartography table anywhere
- `/condense` ‚Äî Convert items to blocks
- `/lay` ‚Äî Lay down
- `/sit` ‚Äî Sit on stairs/slabs

**Plus Hedgehog Exclusives:**
- **Keep Inventory** ‚Äî Keep items on death
- **Instant teleports** ‚Äî No warmup on `/tpa`, `/home`
- **Hedgehog pet** ‚Äî Cute companion follows you around

---

### Squirrel ($25)

Everything from Hedgehog, plus Vote Ranks 6-10 commands:
- `/feed` ‚Äî Restore hunger
- `/loom` ‚Äî Open loom anywhere
- `/heads` ‚Äî Decorative head menu
- `/craft` ‚Äî Open crafting table anywhere
- `/near` ‚Äî See nearby players
- `/armorstand` ‚Äî Armor stand editor
- `/dispose` ‚Äî Trash items quickly
- **Home icons** ‚Äî Customize icons in `/homes` GUI
- **Squirrel pet** ‚Äî Fluffy companion follows you around

---

### Otter ($50)

Everything from Squirrel, plus Vote Ranks 11-15 commands:
- `/bellyflop` ‚Äî Fun emote
- `/stonecutter` ‚Äî Open stonecutter anywhere
- `/jump` ‚Äî Teleport up to 30 blocks where you're looking
- `/grindstone` ‚Äî Open grindstone anywhere
- `/crawl` ‚Äî Fun emote
- **Otter pet** ‚Äî Playful companion follows you around

---

### Owl ($100)

Everything from Otter, plus Vote Ranks 16-20 commands:
- `/anvil` ‚Äî Open anvil anywhere
- `/ptime` ‚Äî Set personal time
- `/smithingtable` ‚Äî Open smithing table anywhere
- `/enderchest` ‚Äî Access ender chest remotely
- `/heal` ‚Äî Full health restore
- **Owl pet** ‚Äî Wise companion follows you around

---

### Phoenix (Subscription)

{% hint style="info" %}
**Phoenix** requires owning the Owl rank first. It is a purely cosmetic subscription rank with no gameplay advantages over Owl.
{% endhint %}

Everything from Owl, plus:
- **Exclusive chat tag** ‚Äî Show your dedication in chat
- **Phoenix pet** ‚Äî Legendary companion follows you around
- **Future cosmetic additions** ‚Äî More cosmetics to come!

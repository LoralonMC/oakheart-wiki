# Smith System Overview

Oakheart features a custom gear progression system that extends beyond vanilla Netherite. Visit **Hammerhand the Smith** at spawn to upgrade your equipment into powerful Reforged and ultimate Hardened gear.

---

## The Progression Path

**All upgrades happen through Hammerhand the Smith**, a gruff dwarf-like NPC at spawn. Each item follows a 3-tier path — click any row to see materials and enchantments.

```smith
```

| Tier | Enchantment Level | Durability |
|------|-------------------|------------|
| **Vanilla Netherite** | Up to V | Repairable |
| **Reforged** | Level VI | Unbreaking IV (add your own Mending) |
| **Hardened** | Level VIII | **Unbreakable** |

{% hint style="success" %}
**Hardened gear never breaks!** It has the built-in Unbreakable property—no Mending needed because it never takes durability damage.
{% endhint %}

---

## Hardened Ingots

Before crafting Hardened gear, you must create **Hardened Ingots**—special alloys crafted at Hammerhand's second shop.

```smithIngots
```

{% hint style="info" %}
**Ingots are expensive!** Each {{item:hardened_netherite_ingot}} costs 27+ Netherite and 108 emeralds. Plan your crafting carefully. There are also {{item:hardened_gold_ingot}} and {{item:hardened_copper_ingot}} used for different gear pieces.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Variant Choices:** When crafting Hardened Axe, Pickaxe, or Shovel, you choose between Fortune V OR Silk Touch variants. When crafting a Hardened Trident, you choose between Loyalty (ranged + Channeling) OR Riptide (mobility). Both variants cost the same — pick based on your needs!
{% endhint %}

---

## Why Efficiency VIII?

Efficiency VIII + Haste II beacon = **instant deepslate mining**

This specific breakpoint makes Hardened tools feel truly endgame. Regular mining becomes effortless.

---

## Tips for New Smiths

1. **Start with tools** — {{item:reforged_pickaxe}} makes farming other materials easier
2. **Expect a journey** — Reforged gear takes weeks, Hardened takes months of gathering
3. **Stockpile emeralds** — You'll need hundreds of {{item:emerald_block}}
4. **Explore all dimensions** — Materials come from Overworld, Nether, End, and structures
5. **Use Token Trader** — Weekly smith materials available for vote tokens
6. **Check crates** — Vote Crate can drop smith materials ({{item:echo_shard}}, {{item:ancient_debris}})
7. **Plan your path** — Decide which Hardened items you want first

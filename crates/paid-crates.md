# Paid Crates

Paid Crates offer premium rewards for players who want to support the server. There are three tiers, each with its own theme and reward quality.

---

## Crate Tiers

| Tier | Name | Theme | Reward Quality |
|------|------|-------|----------------|
| **Tier 1** | Daisy Crate | Cheerful, sunny, simple | Good rewards |
| **Tier 2** | Rose Crate | Classic, beloved, romantic | Better rewards |
| **Tier 3** | Lily Crate | Elegant, rare, precious | Best rewards |

Each tier has unique loot tables with increasingly valuable items. Higher tiers have better chances at rare and exclusive rewards not found in the Vote Crate.

### From Other Sources

- **Vote Crate** — Rare chance to win a Paid Crate Key (Tier 1 at Epic rarity, Tier 2 at Legendary rarity)
- **Rank-up rewards** — Random Crate Keys at milestone vote ranks
- **Prestige rewards** — 1 Random Paid Key per prestige
- **Escalation keys** — Daisy Crate can drop a Rose Key, Rose Crate can drop a Lily Key
- **Special events** — Seasonal giveaways and promotions

## Where to Open

All Paid Crates are opened at the **Crates Pavilion** at spawn. Look for the three themed crate pedestals next to the Vote Crate.

---

## Reward Tiers

Paid Crates have three rarity tiers. The loot tables below show exact per-item percentages — here's the high-level shape of what you'll get:

- **Uncommon** (~80%) — Resources, tools, collectibles, and cosmetic bundles. What most rolls land on.
- **Epic** (~18%) — Tier-exclusive cosmetics: hats, pets, elytra skins, chat tags, join/leave messages. Daisy and Rose crates can also drop the next tier's key here.
- **Legendary** (~2%) — A store rank voucher. The marquee reward of each crate.

{% hint style="info" %}
**How odds work:** Each item within a tier has equal weight. Tiers with more items spread the roll across more rewards — so the *tier total* stays the same, but any single item in a crowded tier is rarer than one in a sparse tier.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
**No emeralds in paid crates** — this is intentional. The emerald economy is protected through spawn shop daily limits, so paid crates use high-value resources like Diamond Blocks, Netherite, and Ancient Debris instead.
{% endhint %}

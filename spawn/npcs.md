# NPC Directory

Meet the residents of Oakheart village! Each NPC has a unique personality and purpose.

---

## Core NPCs

### Elder Oakworth
**Role:** Tutorial Guide
**Location:** By the great oak tree
*"Welcome to Oakheart, young traveler. Let me tell you about our customs..."*

Greets new arrivals and explains the village's ways.

---

### Merchant Bramblewood
**Role:** Vote Token Trader
**Location:** Village Square marketplace area
*"Ah, vote tokens! The lifeblood of our community participation!"*

Trade your Vote Tokens for valuable rewards.

---

### Crystalkeeper Shimmervale
**Role:** Crystal Trader
**Location:** Crates Pavilion
*"These crystals hold the essence of fortune itself..."*

Trade Crate Crystals for guaranteed rewards.

---

## Marketplace Artisans

### Willowbark the Lumberjack
**Role:** Wood & Building Shop
*"Every tree I fell, I plant two more. That's the Oakheart way."*

Buys wood, logs, and building materials.

---

### Seedsower the Farmer
**Role:** Food & Seeds Shop
*"These carrots grew in the finest soil Oakheart has to offer!"*

Buys crops, food, and farming items.

---

### Stonebeard the Miner
**Role:** Ores & Stone Shop
*"Brought me some fine copper, have ye? Worth good emeralds, that is!"*

Buys ores, stone, gems, and mining materials.

---

### Moonwhisper the Alchemist
**Role:** Potions & Rare Items Shop
*"Ah, spider eyes... essential for my midnight brewing sessions."*

Buys brewing ingredients, mob drops, and rare materials.

---

### Tidecaller the Fisherman
**Role:** Fish & Ocean Shop
*"Fresh catch today! The ocean provides, as always."*

Buys fish, kelp, and ocean treasures.

---

## Master Craftsman

### Hammerhand the Smith
**Role:** Custom Gear Forge
**Location:** The Forge
*"Aye, need something forged? Bring me the real materials and I'll make you something proper."*

Creates Reforged gear, Hardened Ingots, and Hardened gear.

---

## Community Services

### Wanderlust the Cartographer
**Role:** Warp Master
**Location:** Ancient Stone Circle
*"Ah, you wish to travel? I know every path worth taking!"*

Helps with player warps and navigation. Stationed at the gateway to the wilds.

---

### Quillfeather the Scribe
**Role:** Land Registry Clerk
**Location:** Corner desk inside Inn/Tavern
*"Every plot must be properly registered, for order brings peace."*

Information about claiming and land management.

---

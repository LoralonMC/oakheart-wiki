# Spawn Village

Welcome to Oakheart — a medieval cottagecore village hidden in the heart of an ancient oak forest.

---

## The Village

Spawn is a cozy village built in a forest clearing, centered around a massive oak tree. Everything you need is within walking distance:

- **5 Marketplace Shops** — Sell items for emeralds
- **Hammerhand's Forge** — Custom gear upgrades
- **Crates Pavilion** — Open Vote and Paid crates
- **Community Services** — Warps, land registry, and more

---

## Key Locations

### Central Hub

- **Great Oak Tree** — Spawn point and village centerpiece
- **Village Square** — Open gathering area
- **Ancient Stone Circle** — Portal to the survival world (`/wild`)

### Marketplace

Five shops surround the central area:

| Shop | Keeper | What They Buy |

| Lumberjack's Workshop | Willowbark | Wood & building materials |
| Farmer's Market | Seedsower | Food & seeds |
| Miner's Depot | Stonebeard | Ores, stone, gems |

### Atmosphere

- **Village Well** — Atmospheric gathering spot
- **Scattered Gardens** — Tended by Rosepetal throughout the village
- **Inn/Tavern** — Run by Pipesmoke, with Quillfeather's desk
- **Small Pond** — Near fisherman's stall, fishing contests
- **Village Square Bulletin Board** — Server announcements from Town Crier Loudvoice

---

## Getting to the Survival World

From spawn, walk to the **Ancient Stone Circle** and step through to teleport to a random location in the wilderness. You can also use `/wild`.

---

## Navigation Tips

1. **Spawn at the oak** — You'll always spawn at the great oak tree
2. **Everything is close** — The whole village is walkable in 10-15 seconds
3. **Clear paths** — Stone paths connect all buildings
4. **Return anytime** — Use `/spawn` to return

## Getting More Claim Chunks

### From Voting

- **Rank-up rewards** — Milestone ranks (5, 10, 15, 20) give bonus Claim Chunks (20 total)
- **Token Trader** — Buy with {{item:vote_token}} at Merchant Bramblewood

### From Emeralds

Visit **Quillfeather the Scribe** at spawn to purchase Claim Chunks with emerald blocks:

| Purchase | Cost |
|----------|------|
| {{item:claim_chunk}} | {{item:emerald_block:40}} (360 emeralds) |

This is a great option if you've been grinding spawn shops and want to expand your territory quickly!

## Using Claim Chunks

Claim Chunk items are physical items in your inventory. **Right-click** the item to permanently add +1 to your claim limit. **You must own a land first** — create one with `/lands create` before using Claim Chunks.

{% hint style="info" %}
Once used, Claim Chunks permanently increase your max limit. If you have unused chunk slots later, you can withdraw them back into physical items (see below).
{% endhint %}

---

## Withdrawing Claim Chunks

Have unused claim slots? You can convert them back into physical {{item:claim_chunk}} items!

1. You must be the **owner** of a land (not just trusted)
2. You need at least one chunk slot that is **not actively claimed**
3. Run `/claimchunk`
4. One unused chunk slot becomes a Claim Chunk item in your inventory

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Minimum 4 chunks** — You can't withdraw below your base 4 chunk limit. Only chunks above that minimum can be converted back into items.
{% endhint %}

This is useful for:
- Trading extra chunks to other players
- Moving chunks between accounts
- Selling chunks you no longer need

# Traders Overview

Oakheart features two special NPC traders who accept unique currencies earned through gameplay.

---

## The Traders

| Trader | NPC | Location | Currency |
|--------|-----|----------|----------|
| **Token Trader** | Merchant Bramblewood | Spawn Marketplace | Vote Tokens |
| **Crystal Trader** | Crystalkeeper Shimmervale | Crates Pavilion | Crate Crystals |

---

## Currencies

### Vote Tokens

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **How to Earn** | 1 token per vote site (5/day max) |
| **Weekly Income** | 35 tokens |
| **Monthly Income** | ~150 tokens |
| **Trade With** | Merchant Bramblewood |

Vote Tokens are the easiest currency to earn—just vote daily! They're designed for practical progression items like claim chunks and smith materials.

### Crate Crystals

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **How to Earn** | Rare drops from crates |
| **Vote Crate Rate** | ~1-2% chance (2 crystals) |
| **Paid Crate Rate** | Higher drop rates |
| **Trade With** | Crystalkeeper Shimmervale |

Crate Crystals act as a **pity system**—even if you're unlucky with crate drops, you'll accumulate crystals to eventually afford guaranteed rewards.

---

## Quick Comparison

| Aspect | Token Trader | Crystal Trader |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| **Focus** | Practical progression | Exclusive cosmetics + pity rewards |
| **Currency Ease** | Easy (voting) | Harder (crate drops) |
| **Weekly Limits** | Yes (smith materials) | No |
| **Best For** | Daily voters, new players | Collectors, crate enthusiasts |
